The Mission

The Obsidian Reapers are the enforcement arm of the Obsidian Accord — a disciplined unit of veteran Torn City operators who take contracts that others won't touch. We don't ask why. We deliver results.

Whether someone crossed you in a war, hit you while you slept, or simply needs to be reminded that Torn City has consequences — we are the consequence. Every contract is handled with precision, tracked from payment to completion, and confirmed before we close the file.

We are not mercenaries without a code. We operate on trust, discretion, and an unblemished record of delivery. Your name stays out of it. The target never knows who sent us.
